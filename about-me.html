About Me

Who are you, anyway?

My family and I moved to Arkansas in July of 2022. We have two beautiful boys. My oldest is nine years old and my youngest will be eight this summer. In our freetime, we enjoy spending time outside; hiking, bike riding, gardening or camping. When the weather is muggy and miserable, we'll go swimming, play board games, or a few rounds of Among Us. My nine-year-old is very interested in coding and programming (I'm trying to keep up with him!) while my youngest loves to collect and solve Rubick's Cubes.

I met my husband through work at an insurance agency. He had moved to Arizona to open a new office and I was the first local employee hired. We hit it off right away and have been inseparable ever since. Before meeting him, I did not know that a relationship could be so supportive, compatable, and fun. I feel truly lucky to have him in my life.

Professionally, I spent three years teaching 8th grade social studies, moved on to insurance sales, worked as a Database Manager for a local newspaper, and then started working remotely for Amazon. I have a bachelors degree from Arizona State University, some post-graduate credits, and am now studying with Correlation One and Harvard online in pursuit of a career as a Full Stack Engineer.

Let's keep in touch!
